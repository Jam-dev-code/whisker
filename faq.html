TOP 3 QUESTIONS:

Most of your questions are already addressed on our website so please take the time to read. We can't respond right away to emails or social media comments.

1. Do you have a branch in XXXXX?

No, we don’t have other branches. We only have one PAWS office and animal shelter, both located on Aurora Blvd. Katipunan Valley, Loyola Heights QC.

For assistance outside of Manila, you may contact your LGU or City Vet’s office.
